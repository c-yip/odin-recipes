Crispy Garlic Roasted Potatoes

Crispy Garlic Roasted Potatoes are a super simple side dish perfect for a lazy meal!

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Wash Yukon Gold Potatoes in cool water thoroughly.
  2. Chop potatoes into wedges.
  3. Combine potatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder into a bowl and mix until all potatoes are coated with olive oil and seasoning.
  4. Place potatoes on a baking sheet and bake potatoes at 400℉ degrees for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip potatoes after 10 minutes and bake for another 10 minutes or until potatoes are browned.
  6. Remove potatoes and enjoy!
