Cereal with Milk

Start your day off with a breakfast that even a child can make!

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk or milk substitute, i.e., almond milk, oat milk, soy milk, etc.

Steps

  1. Fill a medium sized bowl with your chosen cereal (DO NOT FILL BOWL WITH MILK FIRST!!!).
  2. Add milk or milk substitute to cereal bowl until milk approaches the edge of the bowl.
  3. Quickly consume cereal to prevent soggy cereal!
